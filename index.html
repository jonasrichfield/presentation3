Aprann kijan pou w jwenn pwoteksyon pèmanan Ozetazini ak konfyans. Learn how to secure permanent protection in the US with confidence.

Ou gen dwa, menm si ou pa gen papye. You have rights, even if you are undocumented.

Nou konnen bagay yo pa fasil, men gen espwa. Ozetazini, gen lwa ki la pou pwoteje moun ki nan sitiyasyon difisil. Scroll pou wè opsyon ki egziste pou ou. We know things are not easy, but there is hope. In the US, there are laws to protect people in difficult situations. Scroll to see your options.

01. VAWA

Proteksyon pou moun ki viktim abi nan men fanmi yo Protection for survivors of domestic abuse by family members

Ki moun ki mèt li?Who qualifies?

Moun k ap viv Ozetazini ki gen yon mari, yon madanm, yon paran, oubyen yon pitit (ki gen 21 an oswa plis) k ap maltrete yo fizikman oswa nan mo, epi ki se Sitwayen Ameriken oswa ki gen rezidans. People living in the US who are being abused (physically or verbally) by a US citizen or Green Card-holding spouse, parent, or child (21+).

Sa ou bezwen genyen:Basic requirements:

  • — Ou dwe gen relasyon fanmi ki kalifye ak moun k ap maltrete w la.You must have a qualifying family relationship with the abuser.
  • — Ou dwe te viv avèk moun sa a.You must have lived with this person.
  • — Moun nan maltrete w (ou PA bezwen rapò lapolis pou pwouve sa).The person abused you (you do NOT need a police report to prove this).
  • — Ou dwe se yon moun ki gen bon karaktè.You must be a person of good moral character.

Egzanp reyèl:Real Example:

"Mari Marie se yon rezidan Ameriken. Li toujou ap bat li epi li di li si li rele lapolis, y ap depòte l. Marie ka aplike pou VAWA san mari l pa konnen pou li ka jwenn pwòp papye pa li." "Marie's husband is a US resident. He constantly beats her and says if she calls the police, she will be deported. Marie can apply for VAWA without his knowledge to get her own status."

02. U-VISA

Proteksyon pou moun ki viktim krim grav epi ki ede lapolis Protection for survivors of serious crimes who help the police

Ki moun ki mèt li?Who qualifies?

Moun ki te viktim yon krim grav Ozetazini (tankou vyòl, atak ak zam, oswa kidnapin) epi ki te soufri anpil poutèt sa. People who have been victims of a serious crime in the US (like rape, armed assault, or kidnapping) and suffered as a result.

Sa ou bezwen genyen:Basic requirements:

  • — Ou te viktim yon krim grav.You were a victim of a serious crime.
  • — Ou gen enfòmasyon sou krim nan.You have information about the crime.
  • — Ou te ede otorite yo (lapolis, pwokirè, jij, elatriye).You helped authorities (police, prosecutors, judges, etc.).
  • — Otorite yo dwe siyen yon sètifika ki di ou te ede yo.Authorities must sign a certificate confirming your cooperation.

Gade Videyo Saa:Watch This Video:

Egzanp reyèl:Real Example:

"Jean te viktim yon vòl ak zam kote yo te blese l. Li te rele lapolis epi li te ede yo jwenn kriminèl yo. Jean ka aplike pou yon U-Visa pou l ka rete Ozetazini." "Jean was a victim of an armed robbery where he was injured. He called the police and helped them catch the criminals. Jean can apply for a U-Visa to stay in the US."

03. T-VISA

Proteksyon pou moun yo fòse travay (Trafik moun) Protection for survivors of human trafficking (forced labor)

Ki moun ki mèt li?Who qualifies?

Moun yo te fòse, twonpe (kote yo te ba ou manti), oswa menase pou yo fè yo travay oswa fè bagay seksyèl san yo pa vle. People forced, defrauded (tricked/lied to), or coerced into working or performing commercial sex acts against their will.

Sa ou bezwen genyen:Basic requirements:

  • — Ou se yon viktim trafik moun grav.You are a victim of severe human trafficking.
  • — Ou isit Ozetazini poutèt trafik sa a.You are in the US because of this trafficking.
  • — Si w gen 18 an oswa plis, ou dwe ede otorite yo (sòf si w twò twomatize pou w fè sa).If 18+, you must help authorities (UNLESS you are too traumatized to do so).
  • — Si yo depòte w, sa t ap yon gwo tray pou ou.Deportation would cause extreme hardship for you.

Gade Videyo Saa:Watch This Video:

Egzanp reyèl:Real Example:

"Yon moun te di Sarah l ap ba li yon bon djòb Ozetazini. Lè l rive, yo pran paspò l, yo fè l travay 15 èdtan pa jou san peye l, epi yo menase pou yo fè l mal. Sarah ka aplike pou yon T-Visa." "Someone told Sarah they had a good job for her in the US. When she arrived, they took her passport and forced her to work 15 hours a day without pay. Sarah can apply for a T-Visa."

04. ASYLUM

Proteksyon pou moun ki pè retounen nan peyi yo Protection for those who fear returning to their home country

Ki moun ki mèt li?Who qualifies?

Moun ki pè pou yo pa pèdi lavi yo oswa pou yo pa pèsekite yo nan peyi yo poutèt ras yo, relijyon yo, nasyonalite yo, opinyon politik yo, oswa paske yo fè pati yon gwoup espesifik. People who fear for their lives or persecution in their home country due to race, religion, nationality, political opinion, or membership in a specific group.

Sa ou bezwen genyen:Basic requirements:

  • — Ou dwe montre ou gen yon krentif reyèl pou w retounen nan peyi w.You must demonstrate a well-founded fear of returning to your country.
  • — Ou dwe aplike nan mwens pase yon ane depi ou rive Ozetazini (nan pifò ka).You must apply within one year of arrival in the US (in most cases).
  • — Pèsekisyon an dwe fèt pa gouvènman an oswa moun gouvènman an pa ka kontwole.Persecution must be by the government or forces they cannot control.

Gade Videyo Saa:Watch This Video:

Egzanp reyèl:Real Example:

"Lavi Pierre an danje an Ayiti paske li se yon jounalis ki te denonse move bagay k ap pase. Li te oblije sove paske kriminèl t ap chache li. Pierre ka aplike pou Azil." "Pierre's life is in danger in Haiti because he is a journalist who exposed corruption. He had to flee because gangs were hunting him. Pierre can apply for Asylum."

05. FAMILY PETITION

Petisyon Fanmi (mari/madanm/pitit/paran) Family Petition (spouse/children/parents)

Ki moun ki mèt li?Who qualifies?

Moun ki gen fanmi ki gen papye. Moun ki gen Green Card ka aplike sèlman pou mari/madanm ak pitit ki poko marye. Sitwayen Ameriken ka aplike tou pou paran yo, pitit ki marye, ak frè oswa sè yo. People with documented relatives. Green Card holders can only petition for spouses and unmarried children. US Citizens can also petition for parents, married children, and siblings.

Sa ou bezwen genyen:Basic requirements:

  • — Ou dwe gen yon relasyon fanmi lwa a aksepte.You must have a qualifying family relationship.
  • — Fanmi an dwe siyen yon papye ki montre li ka pran swen w finansyèman (Sponsorship).Your relative must file a petition and agree to financially sponsor you.
  • — Fason ou te antre Ozetazini an ka afekte pwosesis la.How you entered the US may affect where and how you process your application.

Gade Videyo Saa:Watch This Video:

Egzanp reyèl:Real Example:

"Madanm Paul se yon Sitwayen Ameriken. Li ka fè yon aplikasyon pou Paul pou Paul ka jwenn rezidans pa li. Sa pèmèt Paul rete ak travay san kè sote." "Paul's wife is a US Citizen. She can sponsor him for a Green Card, allowing him to stay and work legally without fear."

06. SIJS

Pwoteksyon pou timoun ak jèn Protection for children and youth

Ki moun ki mèt li?Who qualifies?

Timoun oswa jèn ki gen mwens pase 21 an (nan kèk leta se 18 an) ki pa gen paran pou okipe yo paske paran yo te abandone yo, maltrete yo, oswa neglije yo. Children or young adults under 21 (18 in some states) who cannot live with one or both parents due to abuse, neglect, or abandonment.

Sa ou bezwen genyen:Basic requirements:

  • — Ou dwe yon timoun oswa yon jèn.You must be a child or youth.
  • — Ou dwe isit Ozetazini kounye a.You must currently be in the US.
  • — Yon jij dwe bay yon lòd ki montre sitiyasyon fanmi w.A state court must find you were abandoned/abused/neglected.
  • — Ou pa dwe marye.You must be unmarried.

Gade Videyo Saa:Watch This Video:

Egzanp reyèl:Real Example:

"Tico rive Ozetazini pou kont li paske manman l te mouri epi papa l te abandone l. Yon jij fanmi rekonèt sa, sa ki pèmèt Tico aplike pou SIJS pou l ka jwenn rezidans." "Tico arrived alone because his parents were unable to care for him. A judge recognized his situation, allowing him to apply for SIJS and obtain legal residency."

TPS SE TANPORÈ, MEN AVNI W PA OBLIJE TANPORÈ. TPS IS TEMPORARY, BUT YOUR FUTURE DOES NOT HAVE TO BE.

TPS bay yon soulajman pou yon ti tan, men politik imigrasyon yo ka chanje nenpòt lè. Chèche konnen lòt chemen legal kounye a ka ede w pran bon desizyon pou w bati yon lavi ki pi solid Ozetazini. TPS provides short-term relief, but immigration policies can change anytime. Exploring other legal pathways now can help you make good decisions to build a more solid life in the US.
